Frequently Asked Questions

What are historical monuments?

Historic monuments are fixed assets that are identifiable because of particular historic, national, regional, local, religious or symbolic significance. They are usually accessible to the general public, and visitors are often charged for admission to the monuments or their vicinity.

What is the purpose of historical monuments?

Memorials and monuments are designed to convey forceful messages about the events or individuals they commemorate. Each has embedded in it a particular perspective, an interpretation, a set of values or judgments.

What kind of information do we get from a historical monument?

A historical monument is a rich source of history. It gives us a sense of wonder and makes us curious to know more about the past connected with it. The study of a monument helps the students construct history through observation. The activity generates intrest in historical buildings and develops observation skills.

Which is the largest historical monument in the world?

Pyramids of Giza immense universal value as they are the largest and most prominent pyramid structures in the world. These mammoth sized pyramids are symbols of the great Egyptian civilization that flourished during the old kingdom in 2686 to 2181 BCE.

Which is the most beautiful monument in the World?

The most famous monument of South America Machu Picchu is the most beautiful monument of the world. Perhaps there would not be a tourist who had not visited it.

Which structure took longest to build?

The Great Wall of China. Possibly the longest-ever building project of all time, construction of the Great Wall was started around 2,000 years ago and remained in the construction phase well into the 16th century AD.

Which country has the most historical monuments

Italy is home to the largest number of historical monuments in the world.
